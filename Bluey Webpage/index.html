About Bluey

Bluey is an Australian Blue Heeler puppy who loves to play and explore the world around her. She lives with her family in Brisbane, Australia, and enjoys spending time with her friends and family.

The show follows the adventures of a lovable & inexhaustible six-year-old puppy, who along with her sister Bingo, parents and friends is learning all about the world.
